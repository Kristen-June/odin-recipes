Cheesy Quinoa

This cheesy quinoa bowl is simple, savory, and the perfect comfort meal.

With only about 5 minutes of hands on time needed, this is a perfect busy week night meal or when you just want a quick meal any time.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup raw quinoa
  • 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp each garlic and onion powder
  • 1 cup kale or spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup milk of choice
  • optional 2 tsp began butter for added richness
  • optional protein of choice

Steps

  1. Bring the first five ingreidents and optional butter to a boil
  2. Once boiling, turn to a simmer and cover for 20 minutes or until fluffy
  3. When done, stir in the kale and milk and return to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently until kale wilts and quinoa reaches your desired texture and thickness
  4. If addig a protein you can cook the protein first or stir it in with the quinoa
  5. Done! Enjoy!
