Tofu Egg

Recipe Description

Tofu egg patties make the perfect addition to any breakfast sandwich. Quick and easy to make, you can have a week's worth ready to go in well under 30 minutes.

Follow along with the recipe below to make your new favorite breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14oz) block firm or extra-firm tofu, pressed
  • 1/4 cup chickpea flour
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon kala namak (black salt)
  • 1/8 teaspoon turmeric (optional, for color
  • 2 tablespoons vegan butter or olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into 4 slices by cutting block in half longways and each of those in half
  2. Whisk together the chickpea flour, nutritional yeast, onion and garlic powder, sea salt, kala namak, and turmeric in a shallow bowl
  3. Dip tofu patties in this mixture thoroughly. Set dredged tofu patties aside which while you work
  4. Dip each tofu patty in the mixture again to ensure a good coating.
  5. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegan butter or oil in a skillet over medium-high heat
  6. Once the pan is preheated, place the tofu patties in the pan
  7. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the bottoms are golden brown and crisp.
  8. Once the first side is golden brown, flip the pieces and cook for another 2-3 minutes until under side is also crisp and browned.
  9. Remove from heat. Season with an extra pinch of kala namak and any additional seasonings.
  10. Enjoy!
